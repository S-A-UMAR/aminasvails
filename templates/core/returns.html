OUR RETURN POLICY

Due to the delicate nature of Mulberry Silk, premium Chiffon, and Cotton fabrics, and for hygienic reasons, we enforce a strict quality return standard. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Amina’s Vails.

Return Timeline

We accept return requests within 14 days of receiving your delivery. The item must be completely unworn, unwashed, in its original packaging, and with all luxury tags intact.

How to Initiate a Return

To start your return, please contact us at returns@aminasvails.com with your order number. Once our quality assurance team inspects the returned item, a refund will be processed back to your original payment rail, or issued as a store credit, according to your preference.
